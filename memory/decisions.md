# Decisions

This document records key decisions made during the project development.

## Decision Log

1. **Architecture Style**: Adopted a modular monolith architecture for version 1 to balance scalability and maintainability.

2. **Tech Stack**: Chose Next.js and NestJS for their robust ecosystem and TypeScript support.

3. **Commerce Foundation**: Inspired by Medusa for its modularity and flexibility in commerce solutions.

4. **Notification System**: Implemented a Novu-style infrastructure for customizable notifications.

5. **AI and Personalization**: Integrated AWS Personalize and Gemini for advanced personalization and AI capabilities.
